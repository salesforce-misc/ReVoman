---
title: Logging
description: Monitor execution through console logs and CI/CD output
---

ReVoman logs all the key operations that happen inside its source code,
including how a step mutates environment variables in its [Pre-req and Post-res scripts](/ReVoman/guides/scripts/).
Watch your console to check what's going on in the execution or troubleshoot from CI/CD logs.

:::tip
[Sample log](https://github.com/salesforce-misc/ReVoman/blob/master/docs/revoman.exe.log) printed during execution
:::

![Monitor Execution](/ReVoman/images/pq-exe-logging.gif)

:::tip
If the execution halts due to any failure, the failure and failed step information can be found in the logs for easy troubleshooting.
:::
